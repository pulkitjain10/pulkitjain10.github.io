Tone

I worked on an app with 4 people for Tufts' annual hackathon - Polyhack, and was a finalist. Our app listened on the conversation in a room and matched it's tone to a song with the same tone. We utilized sentiment analysis to create 3 dimensional vectors (sentiment, magnitude, and amplitude) of the conversation in a room and matched that vector to a similar set of vectors of songs.
